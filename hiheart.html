Enlarged Image with Close Functionality
Hi-Heart!

2023
27.6 * 27.6 * 47.2 inches
beeswax, coral bone, Hi-Hat stand, polypropylene, spray paint, wire

：In the post-human era, as the external expectations towards the meaning of ‘being’ change, the image of human beings is constantly hiding, escaping, and superimposing, the work presents an unclassifiable body.
：In China, the human body is situated in a unique cultural landscape where three civilizations-agricultural, industrial, and post-industrial, are intertwined. The work combines organic, industrial materials and symbols to imagine the meaning and possibilities of the future human body.
